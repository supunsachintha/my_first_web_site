Shazam Fury of the Gods (2023)

"Shazam! Fury of the Gods" is a superhero film sequel to "Shazam!" In the movie, Billy Batson, a foster child with the ability to transform into the adult superhero Shazam, faces new challenges when a group of malevolent ancient beings known as the Seven Deadly Sins escape captivity. As they wreak havoc, Shazam, along with his foster family, must confront their inner demons to harness their powers and save the world. Amidst action-packed battles and humorous moments, the film explores the themes of family, self-discovery, and the true meaning of heroism. Directed by David F. Sandberg, the film delivers a thrilling adventure.
